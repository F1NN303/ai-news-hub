Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

1) What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help websites function properly, improve user experience, and remember your preferences. At AI News Hub, we only use cookies that are essential for login and security.

2) Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies – Required for secure login and authentication (via Auth0). Without them, the Service cannot function.
  • Preference cookies – If you allow, we may store your site theme (light/dark mode) and language preference.
  • No analytics or advertising cookies – We do not use Google Analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies.

3) Managing cookies

You can manage cookies via your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies will prevent login from working.

4) Your consent

By continuing to use the Service, you consent to the use of essential cookies. You can withdraw consent for optional cookies at any time in the preferences.

5) FAQ

Do you use tracking cookies?

No. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.

Will login work without cookies?

No. Authentication requires essential cookies provided by Auth0.

Can I delete cookies?

Yes, via your browser settings. However, you’ll be logged out and may lose preferences.
