Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how AI News Hub (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our website and services (the “Service”).

1) Who we are & how to contact us

We operate the Service known as AI News Hub. If you have a question or wish to exercise your privacy rights, contact us at: service.ainews.hub@gmail.com.

2) Information we collect

  • Account & Profile Data (via Auth0 / Google Sign-In): email, name, profile picture, identity provider ID, timestamps.
  • Technical & Security Data: IP address, device/browser info, pages visited, referrer, error logs, session metadata.
  • User Content: comments or content you submit.
  • Cookies & Local Storage: only necessary authentication/session cookies and tokens for login.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information (health, IDs, precise location).

3) Sources of information

  • Directly from you when you register, log in, or submit content.
  • From Auth0 and Google as authentication providers.
  • Automatically via your browser (basic technical/HTTP data).

4) How we use information

  • Provide and secure authentication/access control.
  • Operate profiles, comments, and features.
  • Enforce Terms, prevent abuse, and protect the Service.
  • Communicate with you (support, notices).
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your data for targeted advertising or profiling.

5) Legal bases for processing (GDPR)

  • Performance of contract (provide account/service).
  • Legitimate interests (security, reliability).
  • Consent (when explicitly requested).

6) Sharing & third parties

  • Auth0 (Okta, Inc.) – authentication.
  • Google – optional login provider.
  • Vercel – hosting infrastructure.
  • Neon/Postgres – database (comments/content).
  • Email delivery – Gmail/Google services.

We do not sell or “share” personal info under CPRA. Data may be disclosed if legally required.

7) International data transfers

Our Service may process data in the United States and where our providers operate. Safeguards like SCCs are applied where required.

8) Data retention

  • Account data: kept while active, deleted on request (subject to legal holds/backups).
  • Security logs: up to 90 days.
  • Backups: time-limited, auto-purged.

9) Your rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw consent.

Email requests to service.ainews.hub@gmail.com.

California (CCPA/CPRA): We do not sell/share data. You have rights to know, delete, correct, and not be discriminated against. Submit requests by email.

10) Cookies & local storage

We use only essential cookies/local storage for Auth0 login. Blocking cookies may break functionality.

11) Security

We use reasonable security measures and rely on Auth0’s model. No system is fully secure.

12) Children’s privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13 (16 in EU). We do not knowingly collect such data.

13) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy. Updates will show a new “Last updated” date.

14) Contact

Questions or requests: service.ainews.hub@gmail.com.
