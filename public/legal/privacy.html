Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

1) Who we are & how to contact us

We operate the Service known as AI News Hub. If you have a question or wish to exercise your privacy rights, contact us at service.ainews.hub@gmail.com.

2) Information we collect

  • Account & Profile Data (via Auth0 and/or Google Sign-In): email address, name, profile picture, identity provider ID (e.g., sub), and timestamps.
  • Technical & Security Data: IP address, device/browser info, pages visited, referrer, error logs, session metadata (primarily for authentication and security).
  • User Content: comments or other content you submit.
  • Cookies & Local Storage: strictly necessary authentication/session cookies or tokens required by Auth0 to keep you signed in. No analytics/ads cookies.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information (e.g., health, precise location, government IDs).

3) Sources of information

  • Directly from you during registration, login, or content submission.
  • From Auth0 and Google as your authentication providers.
  • Automatically via your browser (basic technical/HTTP data).

4) How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and secure authentication and access control.
  • Operate features like profiles and comments.
  • Prevent fraud/abuse and enforce our Terms.
  • Communicate with you (support or important notices).
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your information for targeted advertising or behavioral profiling.

5) Legal bases for processing (GDPR, where applicable)

  • Performance of a contract (to provide your account and the Service).
  • Legitimate interests (security, preventing abuse, reliability) balanced against your rights.
  • Consent where explicitly requested (you can withdraw it).

6) Sharing & third parties

We share data only with providers acting on our behalf:

  • Auth0 (authentication and authorization).
  • Google (when you choose Google Sign-In).
  • Vercel (hosting); Neon or similar (database for comments/content, if used).
  • Email delivery provider (for account/verification emails, if enabled).

We do not sell or “share” personal information as defined by the CPRA. We may disclose data if required by law.

7) International data transfers

The Service is primarily operated in the United States. Your information may be transferred to and processed in the US (and where our providers operate). Where required, our providers use appropriate safeguards.

8) Data retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active; deleted or de-identified upon request (subject to legal holds/backups).
  • Security logs: typically up to 90 days for security and troubleshooting.
  • Backups: time-limited and automatically purged.

9) Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, port, or object to certain processing, and to withdraw consent.

To exercise any right, email service.ainews.hub@gmail.com.

California (CCPA/CPRA): We do not sell/share personal information. You have the rights to know, delete, correct, and to be free from discrimination for exercising your rights.

10) Cookies & local storage

We use only necessary cookies/local storage to enable login and keep you signed in (managed by Auth0). Blocking cookies may break the Service.

11) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures and rely on Auth0’s security model for authentication. No system is perfectly secure.

12) Children’s privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). If you believe a child provided data, contact us to delete it.

13) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy. We will post updates with a new “Last updated” date.

14) Contact

Questions or requests: service.ainews.hub@gmail.com
